Beginner Blend Guide

These blends are educational and not medical advice. Start low, check interactions, and consult a qualified professional if you are pregnant, nursing, taking medications, or managing a health condition.

1) Focus Blend
Ingredients (2-4 herbs): Green Tea, Rosemary, Peppermint
Intended effect: Focus and mental clarity
Why it works: Green tea offers gentle caffeine + L-theanine support, while rosemary and peppermint are traditionally used to help alertness and clear thinking without feeling too intense.

2) Calm Blend
Ingredients (2-4 herbs): Lemon Balm, Chamomile, Tulsi (Holy Basil)
Intended effect: Calm and stress relief
Why it works: Lemon balm and chamomile are classic calming herbs, and tulsi is often used to help the body adapt to daily stress while keeping you grounded.

3) Mood Boost Blend
Ingredients (2-4 herbs): Rose, Hibiscus, Lemon Verbena
Intended effect: Gentle mood uplift
Why it works: Rose and lemon verbena are soothing aromatic herbs that can support emotional balance, while hibiscus adds a bright flavor and a refreshing feel.

4) Sleep Support Blend
Ingredients (2-4 herbs): Chamomile, Lavender, Passionflower
Intended effect: Wind-down and sleep support
Why it works: This trio is commonly used in evening teas to quiet a busy mind and support a smoother transition into restful sleep.

5) Creative Flow Blend
Ingredients (2-4 herbs): Tulsi (Holy Basil), Rosemary, Spearmint
Intended effect: Creative energy and flow
Why it works: Tulsi supports steady energy under stress, rosemary is linked with concentration, and spearmint adds a fresh lift that can help you feel mentally open.

Basic Brewing Tip:
Use 1-2 teaspoons of dried herbs per 8 oz (240 ml) of hot water. Steep 5-10 minutes, strain, and enjoy.

## RED FLAGS (Stop & Reassess If These Happen)

🚩 **No email signups after week 2**
→ Problem: Lead magnet not compelling, or popup not visible
→ Action: Change lead magnet OR improve popup placement

🚩 **Zero affiliate clicks after week 2**
→ Problem: Links not visible, traffic not arriving, or CTA bad
→ Action: Test different CTA copy; ensure links are prominent

🚩 **Missing 3+ weeks of content targets**
→ Problem: You're overcommitted or motivation waning
→ Action: Cut scope in half; focus on one thing; recommit

🚩 **No blog posts ranking after 8 weeks**
→ Problem: Content quality, SEO issues, or wrong keywords
→ Action: Audit keywords; improve internal linking; check on-page SEO

🚩 **Spending >20 hours/week on this**
→ Problem: Scope creep; perfectionism; feature building
→ Action: Re-read the plan; remember: done > perfect

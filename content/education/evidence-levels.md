Not all evidence is equal. This site grades claims so you can tell the difference
between a well-replicated human finding and a hopeful extrapolation.

- **Strong** — multiple randomized controlled trials or meta-analyses in humans.
- **Moderate** — a few human trials, sometimes small or with mixed results.
- **Limited** — early human data, observational signals, or single studies.
- **Preliminary** — mostly mechanism, animal, or traditional-use evidence.

When source data is incomplete, labels stay conservative on purpose. A low grade
does not mean a supplement "does not work" — it means the human evidence is not
yet strong enough to claim that it does.

# Calm Blend Starter Pack

> A practical beginner guide for using **Calm Blend** to support **calm and evening wind-down**.

## 1. What This Blend Is For

Calm Blend is made to help ease mental tension and support a smoother transition into rest mode. It is especially useful when your mind feels busy in the evening or after a stressful day. The goal is steady support, not sedation.

## 2. What’s Inside This Blend

### Chamomile

**Purpose:** Often used to ease nervous tension and support relaxation before bed.

**Simple effect:** Calming

### Lemon Balm

**Purpose:** Helps take the edge off stress and can support a lighter mood.

**Simple effect:** Soothing, gently uplifting

### Passionflower

**Purpose:** Commonly used for racing thoughts and to promote a settled nighttime routine.

**Simple effect:** Quieting, relaxing

## 3. How to Prepare

Use this baseline method:

- Measure **1 teaspoon** total dried herbs per cup
- Add hot water (avoid boiling water for delicate herbs)
- Cover and steep for **10–15 minutes**
- Strain and add optional honey to taste

## 4. How to Use

- **When to take it:** 30–60 minutes before bed or after a stressful evening
- **How often:** 1 cup nightly, up to 2 cups on high-stress days
- **What to expect:** A softer body feel, quieter thoughts, and easier bedtime consistency with regular use

## 5. Safety Notes

- Avoid combining herbs with medications unless you’ve checked with a qualified healthcare professional
- Start with a lower amount to assess personal response
- Listen to your body and pause use if anything feels off

## 6. Pro Tips

- **Stacking idea:** Pair with 5–10 minutes of slow breathing
- **Taste upgrade:** Add honey plus a small slice of orange peel
- **Routine cue:** Brew right after dinner so your body associates it with winding down

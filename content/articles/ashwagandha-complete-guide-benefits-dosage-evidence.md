## Quick Facts

- Scientific Name: *Withania somnifera*
- Used For: Stress reduction, anxiety, sleep quality, strength, cortisol management
- Typical Dosage: 300–600 mg daily standardized extract
- Onset: 2–4 weeks for stress relief

## Introduction

Ashwagandha is one of the most researched adaptogens currently available. Clinical studies suggest benefits for stress reduction, anxiety management, sleep quality, and cortisol regulation when standardized root extracts are used consistently.

## Key Evidence

### Stress & Anxiety

Multiple randomized controlled trials report meaningful reductions in stress perception, anxiety scores, and cortisol levels compared with placebo.

### Sleep Quality

Research suggests improvements in sleep onset, sleep quality, and overall restorative sleep, particularly in people whose sleep disruption is related to stress.

### Strength & Recovery

Several human studies report enhanced strength gains and improved recovery outcomes when combined with resistance training.

## Dosage

- 300 mg/day: conservative starting dose
- 600 mg/day: most common evidence-based dose
- Take with food
- Continue for at least 8 weeks before evaluating efficacy

## Safety

Generally well tolerated. Use caution with pregnancy, certain thyroid conditions, sedative medications, and autoimmune disorders.

## Bottom Line

Ashwagandha is among the strongest evidence-supported herbal supplements for stress and anxiety reduction. The best-supported protocol is a standardized root extract providing 300–600 mg daily for at least 8 weeks.

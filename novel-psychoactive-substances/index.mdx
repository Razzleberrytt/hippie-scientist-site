# Novel Psychoactive Substances

This section provides information on emerging novel psychoactive substances (NPS), with a focus on compounds that have appeared in unregulated markets in recent years.

Many of these substances reach consumers with very limited human safety or efficacy data. The content in this section prioritizes transparency about what is and is not known, while emphasizing harm reduction.

## Important Context

- A significant number of novel psychoactive substances have little to no controlled human clinical data.
- Product composition in unregulated markets can be inconsistent or mislabeled.
- The absence of robust human safety data does not indicate that these substances are low-risk.
- This section is intended to support informed decision-making and harm reduction, not to encourage use.
